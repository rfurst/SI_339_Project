Finland consistently ranks as one of the top countries in education and happiness. Similar to Sweden, there is very little wealth disparity among Finland's citizens. Many Finnish citizens that live in the city find no need for cars due to public transportation being the prevalent form of transportation.

A large part of Finnish culture is the sauna. Due to the cold climate, a popular Finnish pasttime is to repetitively get into the sauna and then plunge into a freezing cold lake. As a whole, Finnish people are stereotyped as being reserved or quiet. Finland also has some globally popular companies that were founded there, including SuperCell (the creators of Angry Birds and Clash of Clans) and Marimekko (fashion brand). The most popular sport in Finland is soccer, but a close second is ice hockey. Historically, the Finnish hockey team is among the top contenders for many international tournaments.
