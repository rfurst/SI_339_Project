Finland is a Nordic country located near Sweden and Norway. It has 5.6 million citizens with its capital, Helsinki, being the most populated city. While Helsinki is a lively, urban area, Finland as a whole is fairly rural. For this reason Finland is a great setting for those who enjoy nature. Finland has a very cold climate, providing very snowy winters as well as a suitable environment for animals like reindeers.

The current prime minister of Finland is Sanna Marin, making Finland one of the few countries with a female prime minister. Finland is a top performer in numerous metrics of national performance, including education, economic competitiveness, civil liberties, quality of life and human development. In 2015, Finland ranked first in the World Human Capital, topped the Press Freedom Index, and was the most stable country in the world during 2011-2016, according to the Fragile States Index; it is second in the Global Gender Gap Report, and has ranked first in every annual World Happiness Report since 2018.
